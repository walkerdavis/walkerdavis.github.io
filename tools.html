Jack the Ripper Drum Loops

This a collection of drums loops I put together from a session I did with Jack Given, of bands X, X, X. They were recored and mixed at 44.1 kHz, in stereo. BPM is listed in the title of each track. Recording was done at CCRMA, Stanford on August 22nd, 2017. Editing, processing, and mixing was done in Ableton Live 9.7 and Logic Pro X 10.3.2. The mics used are as follows:

Snare - Josephson C42
Kick - Electro-Voice N/D868
Overheads - Share KSM-141 pair
Meat - Electro-Voice RE-20s
Room - Neumann TLM-193
